# TripplannerAI

### Project Purpose
TripPlannerAI is an AI-powered travel assistant designed to make trip planning simple and stress-free. It helps users plan their travel itineraries, estimate total costs, and make better decisions about where to stay, eat, and visit â€” all in one place.

---

### Problem
Planning a trip to a new city or country can be overwhelming.  
We often:
- Struggle to organize places to visit in the right order.
- Spend more money than estimated.
- Miss out on must-see spots.
- Face trouble finding parking, food, and the best visiting times.

---

### Solution
TripPlannerAI acts as a smart chatbot that plans an entire trip for you.  

The tool must provides a structured plan with:
- Travel itinerary (optimized by location)
- Suggested restaurants and food types
- Parking options near attractions
- Best visiting hours for each location
- Estimated total cost
